Internships at Continexa

Continexa offers hands-on internship opportunities across our core divisions: Travel & Tours, Technology, and Agricultural & Engineering Solutions. Our program is designed to give students and recent graduates real-world experience, helping them develop skills, gain industry insight, and prepare for successful careers.

Who can apply

University students or recent graduates in relevant fields Motivated individuals with a passion for innovation and learning Team players eager to gain practical experience in a fast-growing, multi-industry company

What you’ll learn

Industry Knowledge: Gain first-hand exposure to operations across travel, technology, agriculture, and engineering. Practical Skills: Learn project management, problem-solving, and technical skills relevant to your field.

How to apply

Send your CV and cover letter to info@continexagroup.com.
